2nd-level evocation 

Casting Time: 1 action

Range:  self

Components: V, S

Duration:instantaneous 

 you take 2d8 blood damage then in a 10ft cone,  send out a wave of blood in a slashing arc all creatures caught in the wave takes 1d10 fire damage and are stunned for 1 round after a creature is caught in a blood wave you can use a bonus action to cast blood beam at 1st level
