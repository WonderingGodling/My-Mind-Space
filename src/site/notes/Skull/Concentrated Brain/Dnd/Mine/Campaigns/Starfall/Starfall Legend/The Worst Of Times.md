# <center><span style="color:#FAE000">The Worst Of Times</span></center>

 I Thought I Would Take This To My Grave But Today I Looked At My Students And Realised That East Phygria Deserved To Have Some Of This Written Down. Part Of Me Is glad I Waited, Archives Have Fallen Left And Right In My Lifetime; Part Of Me Wants To Wait Longer And Allow The Dust In My Lungs To Swallow me Whole.

 Its The Monsters That Weigh So Heavily On My Mind And The Kaijin. They Used To Be More Friendly Before All This And I Fear It Is Our Fault For Not Putting In The Effort And Sitting Idly By As Discrimination Took Root. Before The Stars Fell....

<s>I Have This Harpy Friend</s> <span style="color: transparent;">Just Be Real, Halo. Youll Be Dead By The Time Anyone Reads This.</span>
This Wasnt Controversial When We Were Young But It Is Now Because Of "Educational" Guides And Marked Off territories. My Wife Corax—Whom I Love More Than This Whole University Which I have Built My Life Around—Is A Harpy. With The Most Gorgeous Black Feathers And The Voice Of An Actual Angel. She <s>Is</s> Was A Chosen One Of The Bard Before That Church Imploded. She Is Not The Focus Of This Memo But I Want You To Know How Close My Heart Is To This Situation.

It Used To Be That Monsters Came Into Our World Via Portals The Size Of Dragons. There Were Professionals Who Could Tell When And Where These Portals Would Appear. They Had The Capability To Prognosticate The Nature Of The Other Side And Send People Through To Neutralise The Situation. In The Case That They Were Unable To Solve The Issue Then Monsters Would Spill Out And The Locals Would Be Forced To Handle It. 

Since You-Know-When, Monsters Merely Coalesce On The Spot. Which Has Lead To People Needing To Know How To Protect Themselves From Sudden Appearances Or Overtakings. Using Harpies As An Example: When They Set Up A Territory It Is Important That Young Men Do Not Get Lost Among Them. Because Of This It Is Now Taught As A General Rule That All Harpies Are Men Stealers.

I Know Who Put This In Motion But I Am Not Here To Point Fingers. I Just Hope That Once Im Gone People Will Learn How To Deal With The Monster Problem In A Way That Comes With Less Colterol. The World Was Better Before Those Things intervened So I Know That They They Can Be Better In The Future <span style="color: transparent;">Even If The Only Soluton Is For Those Terrors To Be Consumed By The Monsters They Despise</span>
