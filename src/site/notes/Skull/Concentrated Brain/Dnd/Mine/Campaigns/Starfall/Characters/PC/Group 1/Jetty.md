## You Wont Believe What This Cost Me
ₐ꜀ₜᵤₐₗₗᵧ ᵢ ₖₑₚₜ ₜₕₑ ₗₑ₉

There Is Still A Harsh Divide Between The Human And Demon Parts Of Your Body Despite How Long You've Had It. Every Now And Then The Powers Inside You Grind Against Each Other And As Your Demonic Blood Boils Over You Experience One Of The Following Effects:
- The Next Touch spell You Cast Doesnt Cost A Spell Slot
- By Relinquishing Control Of Your Other Arm You Gain Strength Equal To Your Proficiency Modifier (This Cannot Exceed 20)
	- Unfortunately The Withdrawal Following This Hurts Your Very Being For Hurting You For [Character Level]D6+Strenght

The More Infrequently This Happens The More It Hurts
