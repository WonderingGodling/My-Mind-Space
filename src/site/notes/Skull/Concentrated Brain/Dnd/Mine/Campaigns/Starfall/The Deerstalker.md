# <center><span style="color:#FADFAE">The Deerstalker</span></center>

- Oldest God
- No Inherent physical form
	- Can be Seen MOving s Ooze, Slow, light
	- Often Represented As A Humanoid (Version Of The creature creating The Depiction) With A Hollow Skull Head
		- Sometimes This Skull Has (Compound) Eyes, Or Orbs, Or Just Pupils
- Eye Imagery Is Often Incorporated Into The Art
- Never Spoken
	- Possibly Cant
- Usually Talked About Watching Another God Which Is Where The Name Deer Stalker Came From
	- Used To Be Thought Of As The Judge Of The Gods Until The Judge Of The Gods Actually Became A Thing
- Slowly Became Known Over Time As Smaller And Smaller Pieces Of Information Surfaced
- Can Possess Gods
- Is Harmony
	- Not Just The God Of It But A Projection Of It
