- Golden dragonborn (early 40’s), stout and bottom heavy figure, bleaker and rougher scales and the general vibe of a worn man that’s seen the world. Usually a carefree demeanor, has a simple bluntness towards life.
    
- Hearty merchant that sold many things across his years, travelled a lot through all sorts of places, very fond of his past. Close friend of Roland, starting from selling some tea to multiple meetings and blooming into a fond connection
    
- The one who suggested Everett go out into the world to find some spellcaster to learn more of his condition, in part wishing to help Everett see the world more. Point of inspiration for the sorcerer, always seen as a sort of uncle figure
