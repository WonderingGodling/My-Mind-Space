Delmar is a young, blonde, tall and pretty faced lad. He does have some brownish scales on his face, more all over his body, also a forked tongue, sharp fangs and split pupils. He's wearing worn out fancy blue clothing with random, in his opinion, shitty swords he's found lying around slung at his side. Delmar was the eldest son of a rich family. The heir, only having a younger sister. Both trained in fighting and both taught how to deal with the family name they turned out quite different. Delmar was carefree, sometimes picking drinking, brothels or other tomfoolery to studying, but still, being afraid of their strict father, he kept up with training. His sister Elliana was more diligent, having quick wit and an eye for official "bussiness". They both did get along well though. Together with they're other friends, a well known blacksmiths' son Corwin and a mage girl from a sorcerer family, they got into plenty of trouble. But things took a turn. During a hunt with his 3 friends, Delmar got stabbed in the back of the neck. Before he saw who actually did it, he passed out, waking up in a run down shack in a bloody ritual circle. He was turned from human into a yuan ti. Believing his closest friends had betrayded him he has set out for revenge. He now wishes to become strong enough to face and defeat them for what he believes they have done. He is but a spoiled child, who was now thrown into the harsh real world with basically nothing to his name. He's a bit cocky and unserious, but now there is a certain melancholy and hate filled pessimism to him,he's not evil though. He also wants to avoid getting to much spotlight due to his new snake features, but old habits die hard.
