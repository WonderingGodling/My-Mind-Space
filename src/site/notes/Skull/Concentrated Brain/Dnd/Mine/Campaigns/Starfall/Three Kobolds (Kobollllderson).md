# Notes: 

Self Defense

At 3rd level you gain the ability to defend yourself in comba:
Unarmored Defense: - When wearing no armor, your armor class is 10+Dex+Wis 
Martial Arts: - You gain proficiency in unarmed combat - You can roll a d4 in place of the normal damage of your unarmed strike - When you use the Attack action with an unarmed strike on Your Turn, you can make one unarmed strike as a Bonus Action.

Blameless

At 9th level you have mastered the art of shifting blame off of yourself, and often onto other people.You have advantage on Charisma (Deception) checks to convince someone of your innocence. If your check passes, you can roll a Charisma (Deception) check contested by a Charisma (Persuasion) check to shift the blame to another creature of your choosing.
