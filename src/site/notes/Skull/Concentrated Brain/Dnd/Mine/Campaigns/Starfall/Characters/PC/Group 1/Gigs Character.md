## Rehearsed, Multiversed, Reimbursed
Eeny Meeny Miny You

There Are People In The Multiverse Who Look, Sound, Think And Breathe Identically To __You__. __You__ Can Take One Minute Of Concentration To Find One Of You Who Has A Desired Difference, Allowing __You__ To Change A Proficiency Or Known Spell.

Additionally __You__ Can Take Advantage Of Spells That Have Been Put Into The Multiverse. Casting Spells Spell Is A Similar Experience To Wild Magic But If __You__ Roll A 20 To Control This Surge, Then __You__ Can Use The Spell Cast By A Different You (This Spell Doesnt Take A Spell Slot And Maintains Concentration)
