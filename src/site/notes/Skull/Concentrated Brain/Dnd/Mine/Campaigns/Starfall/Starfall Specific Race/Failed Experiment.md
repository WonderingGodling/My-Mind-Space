# <center><span style="color:#5ed164">Failed Experiment</span></center>


- **Ability Score Increase:** Strength, Dexterity And Constitution +3, Intelligence Wisdom And Charisma Become 3. 
- **Creature Type:** You Are Both Your Birth Type And Monstrosity
- **Age:** You Become Blind To The Passage Of Time, Every Day Feels Like The Same One, your Age Is Forgotten.
- **Size:** You Are One Size Larger Than You Were At Birth
- **Alignment:** Good And Bad Become Meaningless, Attain Your Goals In Any Way Necessary.
- **Speed:** Your Base Walking Speed Becomes 60. You Can Dash As A Bonus Action But Fall Prone At The Start Of Your Next Turn.
- **Language:** You Know Abyssal, Infernal, And Primordial, As Well As The Language Of Your Birth Race (Unless It Is Common)
- **Lurker:** You Gain Sneak Attack And Move Silently If At Half Speed.
- **Corrupted Flesh:** When A Creature Makes Contact With You They Take 1d6 Random Damage Per Level You Are. (Roll A D8 To Determine The Damage: 1 acid, 2 cold, 3 fire, 4 lightning, 5 necrotic, 6 poison, 7 radiant, 8 thunder). Your Unarmoured Defence Also Becomes Strength + Dexterity + Constiution + Proficiency
- **Experimentation:** When You Take Any Type Of Damage You Become Resistant To It Until You Take A Different Type Of Damage
- **Madness:** You Gain A Random Indefinite Madness that Cannot Be Removed. When Reduced To Half Health You Gain One Short-Term Madness. If Reduced To 0 Health You Gain One Health You Gain One Long-Term Madness
- **Instable:** When You Roll A 1 You Take Damage Equal To One Of Your Hit Die And Skip Your Next Action
- **Almost There** The First Time You Are Reduced To 0 Health Per Day, Return To 1 Health And Take An Extra Turn
