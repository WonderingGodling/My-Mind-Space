# <center><span style="color:#D00009">Eye Am</span></center>

I Have Eyes So I Must See
I Have A Brain So I Must Think

Must I Think Of The World I See
Because The World I See Is Of A Blinding Swirl

I Dont Think Much Of The World But Of The People On It
It Pains Me To Think That Theyre Part Of It
Theres Not Much From The World I Expect To Get
Missing The Reward Of Feelings That Make My Eyes Wet

But I'll Keep My Eyes Because Theres More To See
The More I See The More I Beg To Think
