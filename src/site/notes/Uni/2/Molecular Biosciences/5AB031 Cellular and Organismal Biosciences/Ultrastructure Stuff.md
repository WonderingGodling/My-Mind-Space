Ultrastructural Specialisations     
1. vesicles: Some Are translucent, others contained dense material indicating the secretion of hydrophilic substances (Possibly granulocrine secretion) they Are are close to the plasmalemma and Golgi body To transport the polysaccharide materialS 
2. Golgi bodies: play a role in the production of acidic and neutral polysaccharides The Golgi body produces the polysaccharide component which is then transported by the vesicles
3. ER: produces the hydrophilic protein component of the secretory product which is then transferred to the Golgi body And Are periphery To Stalk Cells And along the radial cell wall
4. Chloroplasts: in the stalk cells involved in the production of lipophilic substances (often Used In Traditional Medicine)
	1. large amounts of secretory substances may be produced by chloroplasts
5. Large Vacuoles: Stalk And Radial cells play a role in processing secretory material Not Copmmon In The Cytoplasm Which Is Only In The Periphery Of Radial Cells
