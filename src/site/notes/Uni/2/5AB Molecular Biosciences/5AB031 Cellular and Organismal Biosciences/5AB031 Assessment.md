Exam format
The structure for the exam for this module consists of 50% seen essays and 50% short answer questions.

Short answer questions
You will have one hour in the exam to complete the five SAQs.
For the short answer questions there are five SAQs each worth 10 marks (5 X 10 = 50 marks).
The the topics for the SAQs and set of practice SAQs for four of the topics have been sent to you

Seen essay
You will have one hour in the exam to complete the seen essay.
You will be expected to produce one, logically written, scientific essay chosen from the two titles that will be provided. Each of these essay titles has been written such that you will be required to compare and contrast a particular topic e.g. osmoregulation in plants, animals and microorganisms based upon your lecture notes and additional background reading. This conforms with the learning outcomes for this assessment and for the module. The weighting of
the essay can be equal on all three lifeforms, or 50% on one life form and 25% on the other two.
